About Me

Hi, I’m Jamila a writer, podcaster, artist, and lifelong creator. Ever since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by stories, ideas, and the ways we can use creativity to connect with others and make a difference. That curiosity led me to explore multiple forms of expression: from writing and podcasting to visual art and social projects.
I also love exploring STEM and creative problem-solving, proving to myself and others that curiosity has no boundaries and that art and science can thrive together. Everything I do is connected by one simple idea: using my skills and passions to inspire, create, and leave a positive mark.

"Art is not just creation; it's survival, rebirth, and boundless passion."

My Book

Zubeida Qaryous is a powerful portrait of a young woman fighting to live on her own terms in a world determined to control her. Born into a strict family and thrown into adulthood through a marriage she never chose, Zubeida learns to navigate the harsh realities of tradition, expectation, and survival. Through her battles with family pressure, addiction within her home, and the suffocating weight of judgment, she begins a quiet but courageous rebellion a search for identity and freedom. A short novel with a deep emotional resonance, Zubeida Qaryous speaks to anyone who has ever questioned their place in society, or dared to believe in a life beyond the rules written for them.
